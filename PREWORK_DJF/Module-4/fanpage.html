Alash!

I think we can all agree that Alsash is the coolest Tuvan throat singing ensamble of all time!!!

Reasons Alash rocks harder than any other Tuvan throat singing group...

  • The group and it's members are the recipients of multiple awards and honors, including being the 2004 first prize winners of Tuva's International Xöömei Symposium competition.
  • They remain true to the roots of Tuvan music and their heritage, while being progressive and able to incorporate contemporary sounds into their work. Alash has collaborated with musical artists internationally and across all musical genres. From the beat boxer Shodekeh, to classical ensembles, to Bela Fleck and the Flecktones.
  • They sound as good on the guitar as they do on the doshpuluur.
  • Their rendition of Dekei-oo is the greatest of all time.
  • Ayan-ool Sam, one of the lead singers and doshpuluur players, was named the People's Xöömeizhi of the People's Republic of Tuva in 2015. 'Nuff said.
